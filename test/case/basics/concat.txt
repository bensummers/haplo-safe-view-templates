
concat() is useful for overriding the default behaviour of space separated values in tag attributes.

---
<div>
  <input name=concat("hello[" id "]") value="1">
</div>
---
{
  "id": 42
}
---
<div><input value=1 name="hello[42]"></div>

---

---

---
